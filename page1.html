Our Ciders

Enjoy one of the many ciders from our rotating menu of regular and seasonal offerings, or one of our specialty brews. 

Straight-up Hard Apple

$7
ABV
5.5%

Our go-to hard cider.  Crisp, sweet and appley.  If you just want an apple cider, look no further!

Hopped Hard Perry (Pear Cider) 

$7
ABV 7%

Our Pear Cider is made with Barnett Pears giving it a low acidity, perfect for Hopping.  Enjoy this combination of Mosiac and Simcoe hops and compare to your favorite fruity IPA.

Strawberry Infused Apple Sour

$14 
ABV 6.0%

We add 25 pounds of strawberries to our Straight-up Hard Apple Juice.  The result, our flagship sour.  Just tart enough to be called a sour, just sweet enough to bring out the strawberry, totally crushable.   

Bourbon Barrel Aged Applewine

$12
ABV 14%

Let your sophisticated palate loose on this aged applewine. Sweet apple flavor with hints of plum up front, a full body and smooth caramel finish make this decadent cider a customer favorite.

Belgian Apple Tripel

$10
ABV
12%

Noble hops and Belgian trappist yeast come together in our version of a Belgian Tripel.  Dry apple flavor with just a hint of bitterness and a touch of banana in the finish.  If you like Tripels, you'll love this. 

Spiced English
Holiday Perry

$10
ABV
10%

I love saying Goldings and Fuggles; the hops we use to brew this pear cider version of an English Spiced Beer.  Further adorned with orange peel, star anise, clove, nutmeg and cinnamon, you'll find your favorite holiday flavors in this seasonal warmer.
